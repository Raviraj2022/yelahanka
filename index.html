About Godrej Properties

Godrej Group, established in 1897 during India's Swadeshi movement, has evolved into a global powerhouse with a presence in consumer goods, real estate, appliances, and agriculture, among others. Founded by Ardeshir Godrej, the company now serves over 1.1 billion consumers worldwide and generates more than USD 6 billion in revenue. Godrej Properties, the real estate arm established in 1990, combines the group's legacy of innovation, sustainability, and excellence, with a focus on cutting-edge design and technology.

The company operates across 12 cities in India, managing 92 projects with a saleable area of around 200 million square feet. A public company since 2010, Godrej Properties has received over 400 awards and is committed to environmental, social, and governance initiatives. Core regions of focus include the Mumbai Metropolitan Region, NCR, Pune, and Bengaluru, with a goal of fostering joy through its developments.
